## ICE Calibration

### Impact

| Score | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 10 | Transforms the funnel | Redesigned checkout flow |
| 8-9 | Major improvement | New value proposition above fold |
| 6-7 | Meaningful improvement | Form simplified 6→3 fields |
| 4-5 | Moderate improvement | Better CTA copy |
| 2-3 | Minor improvement | Button color change |
| 1 | Negligible | Moving a link by 10px |

### Confidence

| Score | Evidence basis | Example |
|-------|---------------|---------|
| 10 | Proven in this exact context | Rerunning a winning variant |
| 8-9 | Strong user research + analytics | Heatmaps show 80% miss CTA |
| 6-7 | Industry best practices + analytics | Adding social proof |
| 4-5 | Heuristic expert review | "This copy feels unclear" |
| 2-3 | Educated guess | "Users might prefer video" |
| 1 | Pure speculation | "Let's try purple" |

### Ease

| Score | Effort | Example |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 10 | < 1 hour, text change | CTA copy |
| 8-9 | Few hours, one component | Reordering form fields |
| 6-7 | Half day, multiple components | Adding testimonials section |
| 4-5 | 1-2 days, design + dev | New hero layout |
| 2-3 | 3-5 days | Multi-step form with progress |
| 1 | 1+ week, architectural | New checkout flow |

### Decision Examples

| Change | I | C | E | Score | Action |
|--------|---|---|---|-------|--------|
| Reduce form 8→4 fields | 7 | 8 | 8 | 7.7 | Do first |
| Rewrite hero headline | 6 | 5 | 9 | 6.7 | Do second |
| Add exit-intent popup | 5 | 6 | 7 | 6.0 | Do third |
| Redesign pricing page | 8 | 5 | 2 | 5.0 | Defer |

## Benchmark Conversion Rates

Rough baselines — your own data is always more relevant.

| Page Type | Median | Good | Excellent |
|-----------|--------|------|-----------|
| SaaS landing page | 3-5% | 7-10% | 11%+ |
| E-commerce product | 2-3% | 4-5% | 6%+ |
| Lead gen form | 5-10% | 15-20% | 25%+ |
| Email signup | 1-3% | 5-8% | 10%+ |
| Free trial → paid | 15-25% | 30-40% | 50%+ |
| Checkout completion | 45-55% | 60-70% | 75%+ |
| Pricing → plan select | 10-20% | 25-35% | 40%+ |

**Context matters:** 2% on high-intent organic is worse than 2% on cold paid traffic. Always segment.

## Data Hierarchy

When evidence conflicts, prioritize:

1. **Behavioral data** (what users do) > **Attitudinal data** (what users say)
2. **Quantitative** > **Qualitative** for measuring impact
3. **Qualitative** > **Quantitative** for understanding WHY
4. **Your own data** > **Benchmarks** > **Best practices** > **Expert opinion**
